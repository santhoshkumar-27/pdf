Sodexo is a global outsourcing company working in partnership with public and private clients to design, deliver and support core business operations. In the UK & Ireland, we employee 30,000 colleagues who deliver our solutions from food and catering, facilities management, property and technical services, through to home care; concierge and employee engagement and recognition services. Wherever we operate around the world – be it in corporate workplaces, prisons, universities, schools, military bases, hospitals, or central and local government sites – we are committed to delivering services that achieve value, better outcomes and improve quality of life for the people and communities we serve.

Sodexo has a commitment to creating a healthier, greener, safer and more prosperous society across the UK & Ireland. It sets out the priority areas we are focusing on from our wider social value threeyear strategy. This will not only keep the momentum going of the impact we are making, but also further enhances our focus to meet today’s challenges for a better tomorrow. Deeds speak louder than words, so we also commit to publicly report on our achievements against these pledges on an annual basis – demonstrating with evidence and examples where we are leading the way in creating a positive impact for: Our People, Planet, Places and Partner.

This report will highlight the work we have done during 2021-2022.

partners

An inclusive approach tocreating resilience and growth amongst our partner network.

Our success is built on a wide range of partner networks that contribute to our growth, performance, and effectiveness. A key element of our social value strategy focuses on our commitment to enhancing the capabilities and capacity of our small, medium enterprises (SME), voluntary, charity and social enterprise (VCSE) and Diverse Owned network and suppliers. Our network contributes significantly to local economies through their services, job creation and social mobility ethos. Our strategy includes tangible ways in which we will provide targeted support for these business through our supply chain inclusion programme, which aims to open new markets to SME suppliers, providing Pro-Bono Mentoring support, and through our charity, the Stop Hunger Foundation, as well as many other charitable causes.

Some of the ways we support our partners, include:

  • Hands on, technical and professional support on a voluntary basis that meets charities’ needs, including Mentoring & Coaching
  • Access to education or skills training to enhance their skill base
  • Gifting of our Apprenticeship Levy to support the creation of local jobs
  • Health, wellbeing, resilience interventions, tailored to organisational and individual needs
  • Professional guidance and support to navigate inclusivity of large supply chains

Social Value in Detail

At Sodexo we supplement our impact reporting by defining the Social Return on Investment as a result of the actions of Sodexo, its partners and clients. We do his by leveraging the National TOMs framework (Themes outcomes and measures) to articulate social return on investment for priority measures that are aligned to our Social Impact Commitments.

In partnership with the Social Value Portal, independent validation of our data is undertaken to uphold the transparency and integrity of our reporting. In doing so, we aim to provide confidence for our colleagues, clients and partners that our measurement techniques reflect our efforts, and most importantly, the impact we have on society and our environment.

Our data brings together the way in which we touch our people, our communities, our environment and our partner networks, highlighting the social impact we collectively create. It helps inform how and where we can continue to go further, deeper in terms of creating tangible social change.
